## Melissa (Usage)

Here are some sample dialogues that you can use when talking to Melissa (so much for people not reading Dating Guides).

> who are you?
> how do I look?
> who am I?
> where were you born?

Take notes for later use:
> note some text here 

Retrieve the saved notes:
> show all notes
> all notes
> notes

Retrieve the description of something from Wikipedia:
> define some text here

Ask time:
>what is time
>time

Ask to tell a joke:
>tell me a joke
>tell joke

Play random music:
>play some music
>play music
>music

Play specific music:
>play song's file name

Get weather updates:
>how is weather?
>how's weather?

Open Firefox:
> open Firefox

Drop Melissa home after date:
> go to sleep
> sleep
